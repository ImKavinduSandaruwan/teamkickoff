UEFA Champions League

UEFA Champions League is an association football competition that is conducted anually by the Union of European Football Association, it is been contested by the top European division clubs.The Champions League started in 1992 and was rebranded as the UEFA Champions League in the 1992-93 season. The most reacent championship was held in 2021 in which Italy lifted their second European title by beating England in the finals.


LaLiga

The Campeonato Nacional de Liga de Primera División, commonly known as Primera División, or La Liga is the top men's football division for the spanish football league system and was founded in the year 1929. The 2021-22 season of the championship was taken by Real Madrid and the next season is scheduled to be started on the 12'th of August 2023.

Bundesliga

The Bundesliga, sometimes referred to as the Fußball-Bundesliga or 1. Bundesliga is a professional football league based in Germany. The League was started in the 28'th of July 1962. Bundesliga has a knack for producing home grown, world beating talents like Mario Götze, Thomas Müller and Marco Reus. The 2023 championship was taken by Bayern Munich.

Serie A

The Serie A, officially known as Serie A TIM for sponsorship with TIM is a professional football competition for the top clubs in Italy.The league was founded in the year of 1898. The current champions of the league is the Napoli team and this title happends to be their third title.
